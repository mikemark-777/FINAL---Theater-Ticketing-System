About TicketVerse

TicketVerse is an online ticket booking system on your site which will make it easier for
you to buy and reserve movie tickets. A website that provide the customers facility to book
tickets for a movie online and to gather information about the movies and theaters.


User

Customer needs to register at the site to book tickets to the movie. Users can manage
reserve ticket online, avail promos online and be updated on the upcoming movies.


Our Team

TicketVerse is a user-friendly for customers, the management and for the administrators.
Our team created a user-friendly ticketing system for an easier access to watch the
movies they want in just one click.
